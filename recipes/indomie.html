Indomie Pasta

Description

Indomie pasta is a noodle that is loved by many in Ghana and Nigeria. It is easy to prepare and can be eaten at any time of the day

Ingredients

  • Indomie Noodles
  • Pepper
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • onions
  • vegetables of your choice
  • Eggs
  • Sardines
  • Sausages (if preferred).
  • Gizzard

Steps

Below are the steps in preparing Indomie pasta:

  1. Boil the indomie pasta for about 10 minutes
  2. Sieve and allow to cool for some few minutes
  3. Pour oil in a saucepan for frying and add onions
  4. Whisk eggs and pour into saucepan with hot oil
  5. Add sardines, gizzard and sausages
  6. Add vegetables
  7. Add the indome pasta
  8. Mix and allow to shallow fry for some time
