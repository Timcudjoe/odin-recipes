Cucumber salad

Description

Cucumber salad is a nice delicasy that can be prepared within a short period of time. It is rich in vitamins and fiber.

Per Serving: 50 calories; protein 1.5g; carbohydrates 6.3g; fat 2.6g; cholesterol 7.9mg; sodium 11.6mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • ½ cup reduced-fat sour cream (such as Daisy®)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 3 cucumbers, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced crosswise
  • ½ cup thinly sliced red onion
  • coarse salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

Below are the steps in preparing cucumber salad:

  1. Pour moderate quantity of sour cream in a bowl
  2. Add lemon juice to the sour cream
  3. Dill the resultant mixture together
  4. Add cucumbers and onions
  5. Stir to combine
  6. Season with salt and black pepper
